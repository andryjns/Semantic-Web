Welcome to FC Buitenzorg!

Founded in 1967, the club, nicknamed "Pocong Merah", joined the National Football League the following year and has played at Pajajaran Stadium, Bogor since its formation. FC Buitenzorg established itself as a major force in Indonesian and Asian football in the 1970s and 1980s when Capt. Muslihat led the club to 10 League titles and four Asian trophies. Under the management of Bill Kosasih, FC Buitenzorg became Asian champions for the fifth time in 2004.
